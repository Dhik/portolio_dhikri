Project Overview

DiabetCare is a comprehensive Django-based health management platform designed specifically for diabetes patients. The application leverages artificial intelligence to provide personalized nutrition analysis, AI-generated diabetes-friendly recipes, and an intelligent chatbot for expert health guidance. Built with a focus on Indonesian healthcare standards, DiabetCare empowers users to make informed dietary choices while tracking their health metrics in one integrated platform.

Key Features

Nutrition Analysis

AI-powered OCR extracts food composition from product images and provides detailed nutritional analysis using OpenAI's GPT-3.5-turbo with Indonesian healthcare standards.

Recipe Generator

Creates personalized, budget-friendly diabetes-friendly recipes based on available ingredients, dietary preferences, and user health profile.

Expert Chatbot

Dr. DiabetCare - an intelligent chatbot that provides evidence-based diabetes management advice with conversation history and personalized recommendations.

Health Dashboard

Comprehensive real-time monitoring of blood sugar levels, weight, blood pressure, physical activity, and food consumption with visual analytics.

Diabetes Assessment

Specialized food analysis for diabetes management with glycemic index estimation and personalized portioning recommendations for safe consumption.

Health History

Track and maintain comprehensive health logs including nutrition analyses, recipes, chatbot conversations, and health metrics over time.

Core Functionalities

1. Nutrition Image Analysis

Users upload food product images, which are processed to extract text from nutrition labels. The extracted information is sent to OpenAI GPT-3.5-turbo for comprehensive analysis including:

OCR Text Extraction Nutritional Breakdown Health Rating (1-10) Allergen Detection Age Suitability Consumption Recommendations

2. Diabetes-Specific Analysis

Food products are analyzed specifically for diabetes compatibility with features like:

Glycemic Index Estimation Sugar Content Analysis Fiber Content Evaluation Safe Portion Sizing Blood Sugar Impact Prediction Diabetes Compatibility Score

3. Personalized Recipe Generation

AI generates budget-conscious, diabetes-friendly recipes with:

Budget-Based Filtering Ingredient Matching Nutritional Calculations Cooking Instructions Glycemic Index Targeting Difficulty Assessment

4. AI Health Chatbot

Dr. DiabetCare provides:

Evidence-Based Guidance Conversation Memory Personalized Context Indonesian Healthcare Standards Medical Accuracy Empathetic Responses

Indonesian Healthcare Compliance

DiabetCare is specifically designed with Indonesian healthcare standards in mind:

BPOM Compliance

Follows Indonesian Health Ministry (Permenkes) and BPOM regulations for nutrition information and food safety standards.

Localized Nutrition

Considers Indonesian dietary habits, local ingredient availability, and culturally-appropriate dietary recommendations.

Indonesian Language

All AI responses, nutrition analysis, and recipe guidance provided in Bahasa Indonesia with familiar nutrition terminology.
